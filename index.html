Crispy rice sushi bites

Indulge in the perfect bite-sized delight with our Vegan Crispy Rice Sushi Bites, featuring tofu “salmon” and creamy avocado. These sushi bites offer a satisfying crunch from the crispy rice base, topped with savory, marinated tofu that mimics the rich flavor and texture of salmon.

Fajita bowl

Savor the bold flavors of our Fajita Bowl, loaded with dirty rice, smoky BBQ tofu, sautéed onions and red peppers, creamy guacamole, fresh pico de gallo, and a dollop of tangy sour cream. The hearty dirty rice provides a flavorful base, while the BBQ tofu adds a deliciously savory punch. Sautéed onions and red peppers bring a classic fajita taste, perfectly complemented by the freshness of the guac and pico.

Berry matcha scones

Indulge in the perfect blend of flavors with our vegan strawberry matcha scones. These delightful treats combine the earthy richness of matcha with the sweet, juicy bursts of fresh strawberries, creating a harmonious balance that’s both refreshing and indulgent. Soft and tender on the inside with a slight crisp on the outside, these scones make for an irresistible snack or breakfast option.
