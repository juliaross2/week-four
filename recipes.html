Crispy rice sushi bites

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice: Prepare sushi rice according to package instructions. Mix with rice vinegar, a bit of sugar, and salt while still warm. Let it cool.
  2. Prepare the tofu: Slice tofu into thin strips. Pan-fry in sesame oil until crispy and golden. Season with soy sauce.
  3. Shape the rice: Form the cooled sushi rice into small, bite-sized rectangles.
  4. Assemble: Top each rice bite with a piece of tofu and a slice of avocado. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Fajita bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dirty rice
  • 1 block BBQ tofu, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • Guacamole
  • Pico de gallo
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook the dirty rice according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté the onion and red pepper in a pan until tender.
  3. Warm the BBQ tofu in a pan or microwave.
  4. Assemble the bowl: Start with a base of dirty rice.
  5. Top with sautéed onion

Berry matcha scones

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon matcha powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (or any non-dairy milk)
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, matcha powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the vegan butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the chopped strawberries.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and pat into a 1-inch thick rectangle.
  7. Cut the dough into triangles and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the scones are golden brown.
  9. Let cool before serving.
